8 preventive measures to stay safe online are:-

1. Create a password which is unique and strong such as use uppercase and lowecase characters, use special symbols and even numbers to make it strong.

2. To every app installed on your phone or laptop use two-factor authenication especially on payment apps , bamks apps and social media.

3.  Do not share your OTP or UPI pin to anyone.

4.  Check the receiver's name, shop name and is verified or not before doing any online payment. Confirm with the receiver twice.

5.  Do not open to any links which are being send on sms or your messages by some unknown people. In short, do not open links shared by any unknown people.

6.  If you face any scam or fraud call the helpine 1930 and report it to the National Cyber Crime Petrol and police.

7.  Always log out your passwords and details when you are using public wi-fi. Do not even keep them saved in them.

8.  Try to keep your account private and talk to people whom you know. Do not accept requests from unknown people.
