UPI/ Online Payment Fraud 

Cybercrime is now one of the most and trending topic in today’s world. We daily hear news about cyber crimes happening and almost every age group is facing these issues. One of the major crimes that is being seen is UPI/Online Payment Fraud. Scammers trick people into telling them their personal information or sending them money. It is a part of financial fraud.



A retired old aged uncle gets a phone call from someone pretending to be his son’s friends. He tells him he has got into an accident and his son cards have been declined so he asks him to send him the money on this number. He even told him to send the money urgently as his son was critical and the rest procedure cannot happen without depositing the money. He does not think much before sending the money because it is about his son life and sends him the money immediately.



•	Scammers contact the victims through messages and call.

•	Do acting like being from some bank official or is someone they know.

•	Create a state of panic among victims like your account has been blocked.

•	Sends them unknown links to make them send payments and make the payment link as such as we see in online payments that you cannot tell the difference.

•	Make them reveal their personal information such as OTP’S or UPI pin.

•	Victims are in such a panic state that they do not think once before doing any payment. For them, the first instinct is to not happen something. They make the situation sound so realistic that they even confirm about the situation from others.

•	The entire money has now been transferred to the scammers and now nothing can be done.



The main people who are targeted is-

•	 Students 

•	Elder people who are not aware about technology

•	Young users 

•	The people who have just started using digital payments


The victims lose their entire money and despite of being reported also does not help. There is very less chance of recovery of money and the entire money is gone. It can lead to stress and loss of trust.


Never share your personal information with anyone and do not open every payment links. Use your brain and tackle the situation.
