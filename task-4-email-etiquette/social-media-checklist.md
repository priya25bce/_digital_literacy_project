5 DO'S 

1. Thinking before posting anything on social media.

2. Using strong password and using two-step verification.

3. Check facts from trusted websites and then share them.

4. Talk politely and respectfully to online people.

5. Follow teachers, scholars or the person who teaches about your domain so you can learn from them.

5 DONT'S

1.  Do not post something inappropraite or something bad.

2.  Do not comment bad on other people post while seeing others do it.

3.  Do not share your personal information to the person you are talking online.

4.  Do not open suspicious links immediately.

5.  Use formal language while talking with teachers or researchers. Do not talk with them the way you talk to your friends.
